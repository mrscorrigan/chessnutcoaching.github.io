Who we are

  • Christine Corrigan

    Christine started her coaching career at Chestnut Coaching in 2017. At Chestnut Coaching you will become aware of how to find balance in either your personal or professional life. Christine uses an evidence based approach which will support you in claifying your strengths and enable you to move forward with better outcomes. You will be able to maximize your self-awareness, improve your personal wellbeing and amplify your self-empowerment. Christine has a Masters in Applied Psychology (Coaching Psychology) and presented her findings at the Psychological Society of Ireland Conference in 2015. She is also an affiliate member of the British Psychological Society. She works in Adult Education and has a keen interest in the benefits of mindful walking, enjoys nature photography and music.
